About The Red Hood

The Red Hood, a DC Character, is a vigilante that works in Gotham. He is also known as Jason Todd.

Jason Todd originally worked as a vigilante alongside Batman under the Robin title, however he died during his adventures.

He came back to life and assumed the title of the Red Hood, a known crime lord, becoming an anti-hero of sorts.

This is the Red Hood's full costume.

Relationships

Bruce Wayne

Bruce Wayne, also known as Batman, is Jason Todd's adoptive father. After his death, he fell into a deep depression.

Dick Grayson

Richard 'Dick' Grayson, other name Nightwing, is Jason's elder brother. He was a kind of mentor figure to Jason and grieved him when he died.
